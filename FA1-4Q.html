Animal Welfare Institute

The Animal Welfare Institute (AWI) is dedicated to improve the well-being of animals in commerce, in our homes, communities, in research, and in the wild. We strive to reduce animal suffering and extinction caused by humans such as animal cruelty and destruction of habitats. We need your help to continue our efforts in educating people, and avoiding cases like animal abuse and the destruction of habitats of wild animals.
